Yifei Yuan

Staff Engineer at Alibaba Cloud

Yifei is a Staff Engineer and Researcher in the Network Research team at Alibaba Cloud. He leads the engineering and research efforts for the network verification and validation project. His work centers on developing formal techniques and building robust, large-scale systems to ensure the correctness and reliability of production networks. Yifei holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Pennsylvania and was a postdoctoral researcher at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU).
